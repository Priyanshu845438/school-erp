Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to all your questions about Acadify School ERP

What is Acadify School ERP?
Acadify School ERP is a cloud-based school management platform designed to manage students, staff, fees, exams, attendance, communication, transport, and more from one dashboard.
How much does Acadify cost?
Plans start at ₹990/month for up to 200 students. We provide pricing for schools of all sizes. Visit our pricing page for details.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, you can request a free demo session where we show all features and set up a personalized walkthrough.
How long does implementation take?
Most schools are fully set up within 1–2 weeks. We handle migration, setup, and training.
Do you provide training?
Yes, full staff training is provided at no extra cost — including video tutorials and live sessions.
Is my data secure?
Acadify uses bank-level encryption, secure cloud hosting, automated backups, and strict access control.
Can I access Acadify on mobile?
Yes, we have mobile-friendly portals and apps for teachers, students, parents, and administrators.
What payment methods do you support?
We support UPI, cards, net banking, Razorpay, Paytm, and multiple Indian payment gateways.
Can I migrate data from my old system?
Yes, our team provides free data migration to ensure a smooth transition without losing records.
What kind of support do you offer?
We provide 24/7 customer support via phone, email, and live chat.
Can I customize modules?
Yes, we offer custom module development for enterprise schools with specific needs.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. Billing is monthly and can be cancelled anytime without penalty.
