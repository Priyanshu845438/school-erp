Our Journey

How It All Began

From a simple idea to a global education technology leader

2018

The Vision

Acadify Solution was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to make school management effortless and efficient through technology. Our founders, experienced educators and technology experts, recognized the challenges faced by educational institutions.

2019

First Launch

We launched our flagship School ERP platform with 10 core modules, serving our first 50 educational institutions. Teachers could finally focus on teaching, not paperwork.

2021

Global Expansion

Crossed 200 schools worldwide and expanded our platform to include 16 comprehensive modules covering every aspect of school operations.

2023

AI Integration

Introduced AI-powered analytics and automation features, helping schools make data-driven decisions and streamline administrative tasks even further.

2025

Today

Serving 500+ educational institutions worldwide, managing over 1 million student records, with a 98% customer satisfaction rate. And we're just getting started!
